Learn how to paint miniatures

One stroke at a time

Miniature painting is a meditative hobby that has found itself in the hearts of many hobbyists around the globe. It has massive community that find themselves playing many different types of table top games with little plastic figures. The games look very involved and fun but you find the task of getting into the hobby a monumental task that will take many hours, dollars, and time to bear any fruits. You find that there are many people who are very welcoming and guide you in the right direction of where to get started. Upon going home, you search the internet for a step in the right direction and you soon find that there are so many different guides online spanning from YouTube videos to articles on the web.
